About this locator

Static, client‑side branch list with live search, CSV export, and Leaflet map markers that deep‑link to Google Maps.

This site runs 100% in your browser. No data leaves your device except when you open Google Maps.

  • Search filters both the table and markers in real‑time.
  • “Export CSV” copies the currently visible rows.
  • “Locate Me” centers the map to your current position (permission required).
